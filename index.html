About USC

The University of Southern California is a vibrant, dynamic institution with a rich history. Our campus is a place of academic excellence, cutting-edge research, and diverse student engagement. It’s a place where students from around the world come together to create lasting connections and lifelong memories.

Campus Events

USC hosts a wide variety of events throughout the year, including cultural festivals, sporting events, guest lectures, and academic conferences. There's always something exciting happening on campus, and you're invited to participate!

Student Organizations

At USC, we have over 1,000 student organizations that cater to a wide range of interests—from academic clubs to community service, arts, sports, and more. Get involved, make new friends, and enhance your college experience!

Student Services

USC provides a variety of student services to ensure your success, from career counseling and academic advising to mental health support and financial aid. We're here to help you every step of the way.

Upcoming Events

USC is home to exciting events such as the annual Homecoming celebration, live performances by student groups, sports tournaments, and cultural activities. Stay tuned for our calendar of events!

Past Events

Explore some of our past events, including guest speakers, theater productions, and concerts. USC's event calendar is full of activities that bring together students, faculty, and the greater Los Angeles community.

Academic Resources

USC offers a wide array of academic resources to support student success. Whether you need tutoring, research assistance, or access to our vast library system, we've got you covered.

Campus Facilities

From our state-of-the-art fitness center and library to collaborative study spaces and art studios, USC provides the facilities you need to thrive both academically and personally.
